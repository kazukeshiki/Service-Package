1. Opening & Discovery Recap

INTRO • 5 min
+

Objective

Establish the business context for services organisations, frame document challenges as client risk and revenue risk, set the agenda.

Talking Points

  • Thank the room and reference something specific from discovery — a contract that was hard to locate, a compliance deadline that caused scrambling, a client deliverable that went out with the wrong version, or a staff onboarding bottleneck.
  • \
  • \
  • \
  • Confirm timing: 35 min demo, 5 min Q&A. Ask if there are specific scenarios — contract management, regulatory reporting, or client portal access — they want to make sure you cover.

Actions

  • Open M-Files Desktop Client — logged in as 'Operations Manager - Diana'
  • Show Home View with pinned dashboards: My Active Matters/Projects, Expiring Contracts, Compliance Deadlines, Pending Approvals
  • Have M-Files Web Client open in browser tab for client-facing portal demo
Tip: The language matters in services. Use 'matters' for legal, 'projects' for consulting/engineering, 'policies' for financial services, 'assets' for facilities. Mirror their vocabulary from the first minute.

2. Pain Point 1 — Contract & Document Findability

FINDABILITY • 7 min
+

Objective

Show how metadata-driven search eliminates the shared drive / email attachment problem and makes any document findable in seconds regardless of where it was created or stored.

Talking Points

  • \
  • Pause for their answer. Typical responses: shared drive, email search, 'I ask the person who handled it', SharePoint (if they can find it), personal desktop folders.
  • \
  • \
  • Demonstrate: search 'Service Agreement — Apex Corporation'. Results appear instantly with metadata visible: Client, Document Type, Version, Status, Responsible Person, Expiry Date.
  • \
  • Filter by Status = Active, then by Expiry = next 90 days. \
  • \
  • Open a contract — show the check-out indicator and the lock preventing parallel edits. Show co-authoring notification. \

Actions

  • Use Search bar — search 'Service Agreement Apex Corporation'
  • Show search results with metadata columns: Client, Type, Version, Status, Responsible, Expiry
  • Apply filters: Status = Active, Expiry = next 90 days
  • Show the filtered results — live contract renewal pipeline
  • Open a document — show check-out lock and version indicator
  • Show Version History tab — all previous versions with editor and timestamp
  • Right-click document → Show Related — reveal linked SOW, amendments, correspondence
Tip: If they mention they use SharePoint already: 'M-Files can index and surface your SharePoint content through the same metadata layer — you don't abandon SharePoint, you make it actually findable.'

3. Pain Point 2 — Regulatory Compliance & Policy Management

COMPLIANCE • 9 min
+

Objective

Demonstrate the full policy and procedure lifecycle — draft, review, approval, controlled distribution, mandatory staff acknowledgement, and audit trail — relevant to ISO, financial regulation, data protection, or sector-specific compliance.

Talking Points

  • \
  • \
  • \
  • Create a new policy document from template. Fill metadata: Document Type = Policy, Category = Data Protection, Owner = Compliance, Version = 1.0.
  • Show the automatic workflow assignment: Draft → Legal Review → Compliance Approval → Published.
  • Trigger the review workflow — show Legal receiving a task with the document and a mandatory comment field. Approve and move to Compliance sign-off.
  • \
  • Show the acknowledgement tracking view — green ticks for acknowledged, amber for pending, red for overdue.
  • \
  • Show the Compliance Dashboard: policy coverage, acknowledgement rates, overdue reviews, and upcoming regulatory deadlines all in one view.

Actions

  • File → New Document → Select 'Policy Template'
  • Fill metadata card — Type, Category, Owner, Effective Date
  • Save — show automatic workflow assigned
  • Submit for Legal Review — show reviewer receives task with mandatory comment
  • Approve through Legal → Compliance stages
  • Publish policy — show automatic distribution to staff group
  • Navigate to Acknowledgement Tracking view — green/amber/red per person
  • Open Compliance Dashboard — policy coverage, review calendar, regulatory deadlines
Tip: For financial services: ISO 27001, FCA/SEC compliance, AML policy management. For legal/consulting: ISO 9001 quality management, GDPR. For facilities management: ISO 41001, health & safety policy. Tailor the standard you name to their sector.

5. Pain Point 4 — Knowledge Retention & Staff Transition

KNOWLEDGE • 7 min
+

Objective

Demonstrate how M-Files preserves institutional knowledge, manages handovers when staff leave, and ensures new starters get access to the right documents from day one.

Talking Points

  • \
  • \
  • \
  • Demonstrate the Staff Transition workflow: mark a user as 'Departing'. Show M-Files generating a handover report — all documents, open tasks, and active matters assigned to that person.
  • Reassign ownership to the incoming person in bulk — show all documents and tasks updating instantly.
  • \
  • Show New Starter onboarding: \
  • \

Actions

  • Navigate to User Management → mark user as Departing
  • Show auto-generated Handover Report — all assigned documents, tasks, active matters
  • Bulk-reassign ownership to incoming person
  • Log in as incoming person — show inherited matters view with full document context
  • Show New Starter setup — role-based permissions controlling document access
  • Navigate to Knowledge Base view — precedents, templates, best-practice documents searchable by topic
Tip: This segment resonates strongly with HR Directors and Senior Partners. If those roles are in the room, use a real scenario: 'Imagine your longest-serving account director handed in notice today — how much knowledge risk does that represent?' Then show the solution.

6. Platform Intelligence & Integrations

PLATFORM • 4 min
+

Objective

Show AI-powered classification, Office 365 / Teams integration, and the M-Files API — demonstrate platform breadth quickly.

Talking Points

  • \
  • AI Document Classification: drag in an email attachment — a client contract PDF. Show M-Files reading it, identifying the client name, contract type, and counterparty, and suggesting the correct metadata. \
  • Microsoft 365 Integration: show the M-Files panel inside Outlook. \
  • Reporting: navigate to the Operations Dashboard. Show active matters by client, document turnaround times, compliance coverage, and staff workload by document volume. \

Actions

  • Drag a client contract PDF onto M-Files — show AI metadata suggestion (client, type, counterparty)
  • Open Outlook — show M-Files panel sidebar in inbox
  • File an email against a client matter directly from Outlook
  • Open Word document — show M-Files metadata panel in the ribbon
  • Navigate to Operations Dashboard — active matters, turnaround times, compliance coverage
Tip: If they're heavy Microsoft 365 users, the Outlook and Teams integration is often the 'penny drop' moment. Emphasise: 'Your team works where they already work — M-Files fits around them, not the other way around.'
